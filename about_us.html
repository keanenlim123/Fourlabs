/// Who We Are

About Us

We're building interactive experiences that help people recognize and understand mental health struggles through immersive gameplay and education.

Our Story

Behind Closed Doors began with a simple but powerful question: What if we could teach people to recognize subtle signs of mental health struggles through an engaging experience?

Mental health challenges among young people are rising, yet many never receive support. Often, the signs are present but unnoticed.

This immersive puzzle experience places players in the role of a concerned parent investigating clues about their teen's wellbeing.

Why It Matters

Mental health awareness should be experiential, not passive.

Through observation and puzzle-solving, players identify environmental clues and behavioral warning signs.

Each discovery builds real-world recognition skills.
