Our Story

Behind Closed Doors began with a simple but powerful question: What if we could teach people to recognize the subtle signs of mental health struggles through an experience that's both engaging and educational?

Mental health challenges among young people are rising, yet 60% of those who need help never receive it. Often, the signs are there‚Äîbut they're easy to miss if you don't know what to look for.

We created an immersive first-person puzzle experience that puts you in the shoes of a concerned parent, investigating a teenager's bedroom for clues about their wellbeing. It's more than a game‚Äîit's training for real life.

Why It Matters

Mental health awareness shouldn't be passive‚Äîit should be experiential. Traditional education tells you what to look for. Behind Closed Doors lets you discover it yourself.

Through careful observation, puzzle-solving, and detective work, players learn to identify environmental clues, behavioral changes, and warning signs that might otherwise go unnoticed.

Every object in Ash's bedroom tells part of the story. Every clue teaches a lesson. And every discovery builds the skills needed to recognize these signs in real life.
