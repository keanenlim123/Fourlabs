Quick Answers

Is Behind Closed Doors free to use?

Yes! The basic experience is free for individuals. We also offer free access to verified educators with our complete curriculum package.

What age group is this appropriate for?

Behind Closed Doors is designed for ages 14+. It's ideal for high school health classes, college mental health awareness programs, and community education.

How long does the experience take?

The core experience takes 20-30 minutes. With guided discussion and follow-up activities, a complete lesson can range from 45-90 minutes.

Is this experience evidence-based?

Yes. All content has been developed and reviewed by licensed mental health professionals and is based on current research in mental health recognition and education.

Can I use this for my research or publication?

We welcome academic partnerships. Please contact us at info@behindcloseddoors.org to discuss collaboration opportunities.
