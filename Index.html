/// Mission Objectives

Your Goals

This immersive puzzle experience teaches you to recognize the hidden signs of mental health struggles through careful observation and detective work.

01

Unique Perspective

Experience the concern of a parent investigating their child's private space. Navigate the delicate balance between care and privacy.

02

Raise Awareness

Bring attention to youth mental health through an interactive puzzle that creates emotional connection and understanding.

03

Recognize Signs

Learn to identify environmental clues that indicate someone may be silently struggling with their mental health.

04

Public Education

Serve as a powerful tool for schools, families, and communities to learn about mental health disorders.
